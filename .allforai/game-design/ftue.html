教えるのではなく、気づかせる。
指示の言葉は一言も使わない。
Don't teach — let them discover. Not a single word of instruction.

The FTUE for Glow Island treats the player as a thoughtful adult arriving somewhere new. Mechanics are introduced entirely through spatial arrangement, visual affordance, and immediate sensory feedback. The island teaches itself.

⊘ NO popup overlays  ·  NO forced-tap arrows  ·  NO skip buttons on mandatory screens  ·  NO instruction text

05 · No-Popup Teaching Methodology
Visual Affordance Inventory

Every piece of information the player needs is communicated through visual or auditory affordances embedded in the game state itself. No popup windows. No arrows. No instruction text.

Prohibited Patterns
  • 「タップしてください」
  • 「ここを押してください」
  • Any text with the word チュートリアル
  • 「最初は…」 opening constructions
  • Exclamation marks in tutorial copy
  • Text that presumes player confusion
  • Arrows pointing at UI elements
  • Grayed-out UI with forced tap targets
✦
tile_touch_glow
Trigger: player taps any puzzle tile
Confirms interactivity. The tile is 'alive'. No instruction needed to understand the game has begun.
Effect

Soft teal glow, +40% brightness, 0.5s pulse. Wooden hollow-bamboo tap sound. Single note.

···
path_preview_dotted
Trigger: second tile of matching type is tapped
Shows the connection route before commitment. Teaches BFS ≤2-turn routing through spatial visualization, not explanation.
Effect

Warm ivory dotted path line along valid route. No audio — the visual is the message. Absence of line for invalid routes teaches by difference.

⊙
gap_glow_on_failed_path
Trigger: player attempts connection through an obstacle
Points attention to the blocker without text. The gap is its own explanation once highlighted.
Effect

Obstacle cell glows muted amber-orange for 0.8s. Stone-in-water audio (not a buzzer). Calm and diagnostic, not punishing.

↓
tile_settle_animation
Trigger: any pair successfully cleared
Guides eye to remaining matches through relative brightness. Board 'breathes' between moves — living rhythm.
Effect

All tiles shift 2-3px settle movement. Tiles with clear line-of-sight to their match brighten slightly relative to others.

×2
combo_particle_trail
Trigger: second pair cleared within 2000ms of first
Rewards speed intrinsically without demanding it. Player discovers that rapid clearing has different sensory quality.
Effect

Brighter particle trail on cleared tiles. Teal '×2' numeral floats up, fades over 1s. Board border briefly brightens. Harmonic second note added to chime.

🌊
restoration_world_pulse
Trigger: any level complete
Links puzzle completion to world change before the explicit restoration cutscene. The connection is felt first, then shown.
Effect

Background shifts +5% warmer saturation. Ambient wave sound rises then settles — island exhaling. Accumulates across levels.

ひな
hinata_passive_presence
Trigger: Level 1-3 completion restoration cutscene
Emotional affordance — signals that the player's actions are witnessed by someone who matters. Not reward, not acknowledgment — just presence.
Effect

ひなた in background, mid-distance, not highlighted. Single wind-chime note coincides with her glance at 律.

浮
material_rise_animation
Trigger: first 浮木 material reward after Level 1-3
Makes material economy tangible, not transactional. The wood rising from the dock it helped restore closes a visual metaphor loop.
Effect

Material floats from dock boards to inventory UI corner with warm glow. Wind-chime 3-note ascent. Ripple effect on landing.

⟳
idle_urgency_pulse
Trigger: selected tile untouched for 8 seconds
Gentle invitation to act — not instruction, not punishment. The tile becomes slightly more energetic without telling the player what to do.
Effect

Pulse period shortens from 0.5s to 0.3s. Matching pair tile brightens very slightly. No audio. Resets if player taps anything.

08 · D1 Retention Setup — By End of FTUE

The FTUE is complete when the player has experienced all four retention anchors. Each anchor is a functional hook that creates a reason to return the next day. Together they establish the full emotional contract of the game.

✓
First Island Restoration
Harbor dock partially restored — visible color change on island map. Player has caused a physical change in the world. The loop is proven.
✓
ひなた Seen Twice
Step 2 (letter hand-off) + Step 6 (post-restoration glance). Two brief encounters with appropriate distance — intrigue established, not resolved.
✓
First Material Reward
浮木 (driftwood) collected. Material economy introduced without tutorial. The connection between puzzle → material → restoration is physically demonstrated.
✓
Island Map Unlocked
One restored area visible (harbor, warm amber). Adjacent grey areas hint at unexplored potential. Player sees the meta-loop's scale. The 'what do I do next session' question is answered visually.
Next Session Pull

The island map after FTUE shows 2 grey areas adjacent to the restored harbor — visible but inaccessible. The player knows: more puzzles = more restoration. ひなた has been seen but not known. The letter from 渉 has been received but not yet understood. All three hooks point forward without urgency.

Emotional Hooks Established
律 has a purpose
Restore the island per the letter. Agency without pressure.
ひなた is present but not waiting
She noticed. She does not follow. The distance is compelling.
The loop is proven
Puzzle → material → world change. Demonstrated without words.
The island was beautiful once
And can be again. The restored harbor is the proof.
Appendix · Narrative Context
Protagonist
律
山田律 (Yamada Ritsu)
29 · Ad Creative Director · Tokyo burnout
Five years of advertising exhaustion. Received a mysterious ink-brushed letter from lighthouse keeper Maruyama Wataru. Takes leave and arrives by ferry. Carries a battered leather satchel. Writes in a small diary — precise, circling, never directly about himself.
Romantic Interest
ひな
丸山ひなた (Maruyama Hinata)
24 · Design student · Returned from Osaka same day
Granddaughter of lighthouse keeper. Left island 3 years ago to study design. Returned on the same ferry as 律 — coincidence or something more. Outwardly bright, inwardly deep. Communicates through action. The fisherman's ring on a cord at her chest is the emotional tell.
Setting
輝島 (Kagayaki-jima) — a small island between Kyushu and the Nansei chain, contemporary era. Once vibrant, now quiet and overgrown since the lighthouse keeper's passing. The island's visual saturation is at 0% at game start — each restoration raises it one step. The game's visual arc is the island coming back to color.
The Letter
A letter from 丸山渉 — the lighthouse keeper. Ink-brushed. Addressed to 律 specifically. Contains only the instruction: 「島を頼む」 — "Take care of the island." Why 律 was chosen, how 渉 knew him, what exactly taking care means: all unanswered in the FTUE. These are the questions that drive sessions 2 onward.
