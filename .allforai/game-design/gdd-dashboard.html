Adaptive 3-Layer System Real-Time Engagement Response
Layer 1: BASE
Always playing
Ambient/environmental sounds specific to each chapter area — ocean waves (Ch1), pottery wheel hum (Ch2), wind+birdsong (Ch3), silence+room tone (Ch4), cliff wind+pads (Ch5), full ocean (Ch6). Never silent even in low-resource mode.
Layer 2: MELODY
Fade in after 10s active engagement · fade out on pause (5s)
Primary musical melody — acoustic guitar (Ch1), koto patterns (Ch2), piano (Ch3), solo instrument (Ch4), koto intermittent (Ch5), dynamic swell (Ch6). Rewards continued engagement. Volume target: 80% of full.
Layer 3: INTENSITY
3+ consecutive combos → fade in (0.5s) · fade out 3s after last combo
Subtle additional texture activated by combo chains — soft percussion (Ch1), hand percussion (Ch2), guitar harmonics (Ch3), second instrument (Ch4), piano swell (Ch5), full ensemble join (Ch6). Should feel like the music "notices" the combo, not react frantically. Volume target: 60% of full.
Chapter transition policy: 2-second crossfade, never hard-cut. Lighthouse finale exception: always fades in from near-silence — no previous music overlap.
Voice style: No full voice acting. Japanese wordless minimal murmur. ひなた max 2 syllables (律さん… / うん / ふふ). Protagonist Ritsu: text-only, ambient swells during reflection. 丸山渉: no voice ever — ambient music IS his voice.
